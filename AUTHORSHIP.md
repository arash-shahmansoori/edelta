# Authorship and Attribution

## Original Research Statement

E∆-MHC-Geo is an original research project authored and maintained by **Arash Shahmansoori**.

The repository contains the implementation, experiments, figures, analysis, and manuscript materials for the E∆-MHC-Geo Transformer: an adaptive geodesic residual architecture combining data-dependent Cayley rotations, Householder reflection access, and learned operator selection.
